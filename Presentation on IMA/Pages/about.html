    Ijero First Transformational Leader.

    Captain Bolaji Agbelusi is a social entrepreneur; founder of Dzuels Foundation, an organization concerned with education support and human capacity building for Nigerian children and youth, amongst other social concerns. She is an Oakseed of the Institute for National Transformation and an accredited Management Consultant & Trainer.

    She is married with children to Ven. Adeyemi Agbelusi, a priest of the Church of Nigeria (Anglican Communion), with whom she has served for over 20years building parishes, overseeing regional churches, directly responsible for the spiritual formation of women, youth and children, and assisting to provide scholarly support and capacity building for Christian clergy.

    She currently assists to build scholarly support for Hausa priests at St. Francis College of Theology, Wusasa Zaria, Kaduna State, where her husband is currently the Director of School of Post-Graduate Studies.

    She is a mother to every serious and disciplined child.
