    Capt. Bolaji Agbelusi

    Bolaji Agbelusi (nee Dada) is a Professional Pilot with over two decades of experience in the Aviation industry. Capt. Bolaji Agbelusi made history when she commanded Nigeria's first all-female flight on April 1st, 2009 on Aero Contractors Dash-8 aircraft which flew with passengers from Lagos to Warri and return.
